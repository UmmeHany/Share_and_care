Welcome To Care & Share

People throw away 160 billion tons of food every year with which the lives of many people can be saved.Across the world, there are restaurants that waste tons of excess food everyday.This application establishes a link between restaurants and the charity homes/orphanages to enable excess food donation.The main theme of this application is to provide the necessary information of the leftover foods of various restaurants to the orphanages.

All the restaurants and the orphanages will be allowed to open an account.All the account holder can post a comment in the comment session.Google maps will help the users to find out any restaurants or orphanages.The informations of all the users will be available in the application.

The application will greatly be helpful for the needy people and will reduce the wastage of food around the world
